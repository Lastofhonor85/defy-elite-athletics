About Me

I’m John Smith, lead coach and founder of Defy Elite Athletics. With over a decade of experience working alongside competitive golfers, high‐level athletes, and performance specialists, my mission is to bridge the gap between sports science and real‐world training. I hold a Master’s in Exercise Physiology, am a certified CSCS, and have been featured in Golf Digest for my work in swing biomechanics.

Services

  • Online Coaching: Tailored 12‐week programs covering strength & conditioning, mobility, and golf‐specific biomechanics. Everything delivered via our secure portal.
  • In‐Person Training: One‐on‐one sessions at our Long Beach facility, focusing on power development, injury prevention, and performance analytics.
  • Performance Assessments: Video motion‐capture analysis, force‐plate testing, and neuromuscular screening to identify weaknesses and track improvements.
  • Nutrition & Recovery: Customized meal plans, supplement protocols, and guided recovery strategies—including sleep optimization and mobility routines.
