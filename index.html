About Me

I’m Steven Edward Kittinger Jr., an ACSM‐certified personal trainer with a Bachelor’s in Sports & Recreation Sciences. I’ve coached a wide range of clients—everything from family members (my niece and sister-in-law) to military personnel on an Army installation— plus worked in commercial gyms. Early in my career I served as Athletic Director for the Boys & Girls Clubs of SWC and oversaw recreation programs for the U.S. Army.

Growing up playing every sport since age three—basketball, baseball, soccer, golf, and football—I later refereed and coached youth teams in each. My two daughters have thrived under my guidance in competitive junior golf, which inspired me to launch Defy Elite Athletics and bring my experience to a broader audience.

Whether you’re an elite golfer chasing lower handicaps or someone looking to improve overall health, I combine hands-on coaching with science-backed programming to help you defy limits and reach your personal best.

Services

  • One-on-One Coaching: Personalized swing analysis, functional training, and mental-skills development tailored to your game.
  • Remote Programming: Comprehensive monthly plans delivered digitally, complete with video feedback and weekly check-ins.
  • Performance Analysis: Advanced force-plate testing, trackman launch-monitor sessions, and data-driven insights to optimize your swing and movement patterns.
  • Group Clinics & Workshops: Small-group, in-person training on movement quality, golf-specific strength, and on-course strategy.
