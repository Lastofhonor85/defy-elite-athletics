About Me

I’m Steven Edward Kittinger Jr., an ACSM-certified personal trainer with a Bachelor’s in Sports & Recreation Sciences. I’ve coached a broad spectrum of clients—from family members (my niece and sister-in-law) to military personnel on an Army installation—and have experience in both commercial gyms and youth recreation. Early in my career, I served as Athletic Director for the Boys & Girls Clubs of SWC and oversaw recreation programs for the U.S. Army.

Having played every sport since age three—basketball, baseball, soccer, golf, and football—I later coached and refereed youth teams in each. My daughters’ success in competitive junior golf under my guidance inspired me to launch Defy Elite Athletics and bring my experience to a wider community.

Whether you’re an elite golfer seeking lower handicaps or someone pursuing better overall fitness, I combine hands-on coaching with science-driven programming to help you defy limits and reach your personal best.

Services

  • One-on-One Live Golf Coaching: In-person sessions focusing on swing mechanics, short game proficiency, and course management tailored to your individual needs.
  • Remote Golf Coaching: Customized monthly programs with video-based swing analysis, detailed feedback, and weekly check-ins to keep you accountable from anywhere in the world.
  • 3-, 6-, and 9-Month Periodization Plans: Structured training phases that build strength, power, and endurance progressively—so you peak at the right time for tournaments.
  • Full Swing Analysis Package: Comprehensive TrackMan data review, slow-motion video breakdown, and a four-week practice plan of drills designed to optimize your sequence and speed.
  • Nutritional Counseling: Personalized meal recommendations, supplement guidelines, and hydration strategies to fuel recovery and performance on and off the course.
  • Members Area Access: Exclusive entry to cutting-edge articles and industry news, performance tools and trackers, a complete exercise and drills library, and handpicked performance gear offerings.
