About Coach Steven Kittinger Jr.

Steven Kittinger Jr. holds a Bachelor's degree in Sports and Recreation Management and is a certified ACSM personal trainer. With over eight years of experience as an athletic coach, CrossFit trainer, Army recreation assistant, and competitive golf coach, he has dedicated his career to empowering others to achieve their peak athletic potential. Steven is passionate about family, sport, and holistic self-improvement.
