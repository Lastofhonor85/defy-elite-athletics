About Me

I’m Steven Edward Kittinger Jr., an ACSM‐certified personal trainer with a Bachelor’s in Sports & Recreation Sciences. I’ve coached a broad spectrum of clients—from family members (my niece and sister‐in‐law) to military personnel on an Army installation— and have experience in both commercial gyms and youth recreation. Early in my career, I served as Athletic Director for the Boys & Girls Clubs of SWC and oversaw recreation programs for the U.S. Army.

From age three, I’ve been involved in organized sports—basketball, baseball, soccer, and football —and eventually went on to coach and referee youth teams in all of them. Watching my girls thrive in competitive junior golf under my mentorship inspired me to create Defy Elite Athletics and share my expertise with a broader audience.

Whether you’re an elite golfer seeking lower handicaps or someone pursuing better overall fitness, I combine hands‐on coaching with science‐driven programming to help you defy limits and reach your personal best.

Services

  • One‐on‐One Live Golf Coaching: Personalized in‐person swing analysis and short‐game instruction to sharpen your on‐course skill.
  • Remote Golf Coaching: Weekly digital lessons, video‐based swing breakdowns, and online check‐ins to keep you progressing no matter where you train.
  • 3-/6-/9‐Month Periodization Plans: Structured, long‐term training cycles designed to peak your performance at key tournaments or seasons.
  • Full Swing Analysis Package: In‐depth video analysis, TrackMan session data, instructional drills, and a custom 4‐week practice plan to optimize your launch and ball flight.
  • Nutritional Counseling: Sport‐specific meal planning, supplementation guidance, and performance‐fuel strategies to help you train and recover smarter.
