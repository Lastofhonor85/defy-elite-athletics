About Defy Elite Athletics

Steven Edward Kittinger Jr.
ACSM-Certified Personal Trainer | Golf Instructor | Educator

I’m Steven Edward Kittinger Jr., a certified personal trainer, golf instructor, and educator dedicated to helping people grow stronger—physically, mentally, and personally.

With over 7 years in the fitness industry (including roles in the military, CrossFit, commercial gyms, and youth athletic programs), I combine grit, strategy, and practical experience in every aspect of my coaching.

I hold an ACSM personal trainer certification and a degree in Sports and Recreation Management. My experience spans from teaching English online and running youth leagues, to coaching my girls in competitive junior golf and building customized fitness plans for all ages and needs.
